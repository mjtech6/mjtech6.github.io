What Actually Chrono Trigger is?

It is an JRPG game (Japanese Roleplaying Game). Why 'Japanese'? Because PC RPGs like Baldurs Gate or Fallout or even Diablo were very different from RPG made in Japan for consoles. I'm not sure how to put it but JRPGs had more manga like characters, they were always about saving the whole world, 2D (at least till Final Fantasy VII) graphics that weren't supposed to look natural, different battle system. PC RPGs were an evolution of pen and paper systems like D&D. Simply different vibe.

In Chrono Trigger you begin by meeting a girl and helping her find her pendant and you end up time traveling and saving the world. The battle system is great, story is amazing and music is out of this world.
